What is hard-disk queue depth?

2010-11-13 11:26:58   查看原文>>

1. The queue depth is the amount of outstanding read and/or write requests waiting to access the hard drive.
2. hard-disk queue depth is the time it take a Hard drive in a piece of a electronic device (ex; A Desktop compter) to process a program without freezing or locking up.
